## **Why companies no longer prefer Vervoe**

While Vervoe still delivers solid screening capabilities, these recurring pain points are leading companies to explore Vervoe alternatives that better match their evolving hiring needs.

* **Assessment setup complexity:** Although Vervoe offers AI-driven assessments, many teams find that building truly tailored tests requires more manual configuration and expertise than expected, making it harder for smaller teams to get started efficiently.
* **Template limitations:** Users report that while there is a range of templates, role-specific and customizable test libraries can feel insufficient, forcing recruiters to spend extra time building assessments from scratch.
* **Pricing vs. value for volume hiring:** For companies with high candidate volumes, the cost per assessment can add up quickly without proportional return in capabilities compared with other platforms.
