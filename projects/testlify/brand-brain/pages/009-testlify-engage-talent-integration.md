Engage Talent is a software company that provides a cloud-based, artificial intelligence-powered recruiting platform. The platform aims to help companies automate and streamline the recruiting process and improve the candidate experience.

To simplify your sourcing and hiring process, ENGAGE interfaces with over a hundred ATS, CRM, contact management, and campaign management platforms, including Bullhorn. There are countless ways to customize how it fits your workflow and improve your passive candidate recruiting process by utilizing a solid workflow management system.

* Automated resume screening and candidate matching
* Automated communication and scheduling
* Integration with job boards and social media platforms
* Data-driven insights and analytics
* Candidate relationship management
* Talent pool management
* Personalized career sites
* Video interviewing
* Diversity and inclusion tracking
* Compliance management
* API and Zapier integration.
