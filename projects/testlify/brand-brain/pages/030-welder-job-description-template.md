## Welder job summary

You’ll be responsible for joining and fabricating metal parts as a welder. You’ll use welding techniques to repair, maintain, and build structures, machines, and equipment. Your skills and attention to detail are crucial in creating solid and durable welds that meet industry standards and client expectations.

## Welder roles and responsibilities

* Use welding techniques, such as gas metal arc welding, flux-cored arc welding, and gas tungsten arc welding, to join and fabricate metal parts
* Follow blueprints, technical drawings, and instructions to determine the appropriate welding method.
* Repair, maintain, and build structures, machines, and equipment using welding techniques.
* Inspect completed welds to ensure they meet industry standards and client expectations
* Maintain a clean and safe work environment
* Follow all safety guidelines and procedures
