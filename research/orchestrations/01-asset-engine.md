## Goal
Publish a genuinely useful free asset (calculator, glossary, data study, etc.) that other sites
choose to link to — then route that authority to your commercial pages.

## When to run it
You have budget/skill for a high-effort asset and want links that compound over time, not a one-off.
This is the core content-driven campaign the others feed into.

## The sequence
1. **Health check** → [[strategies/toxic-backlink-audit]] — _flag any toxic existing links so you build on a clean foundation._
2. **Generate ideas** → [[strategies/link-bait-generation-methods]] — _brainstorm ~100 link-worthy ideas using the 3 research methods (competitor study, model other niches, trends)._
3. **Pick a format** → [[strategies/link-bait-asset-types]] — _choose a proven format from the catalog (calculator, glossary, quiz, facts page, original research…)._
4. **Validate + build** → [[strategies/linkable-asset-creation]] — _confirm the idea has proven link demand (linking-root-domains signal), then build it with real depth._
5. **Launch it** → [[strategies/linkable-asset-promotion]] — _internal-link to it first, then run outreach. Outreach uses [[strategies/link-prospecting-prioritization]] to find/rank targets and [[strategies/outreach-value-proposition]] for the pitch._
6. **Run the outreach properly** → [[strategies/outreach-execution]] — _the operational layer: find + verify contacts, warm the inbox, send, track, and follow up (the spine under every pitch)._ Keep anchors safe with [[strategies/anchor-text-optimization]] — _branded/varied, never exact-match-heavy._
   - For *systematic* promotion channels (roundups, list posts, resource pages, guest posts), hand off to [[orchestrations/05-content-promotion-outreach]].
7. **Structure the equity** → [[strategies/reverse-silo]] — _point external links at the asset; internally link the asset to your money pages._

## Where the links point
External backlinks aim at the **asset**, never directly at money pages. The asset then passes value
internally to commercial pages (the reverse-silo). See step 6.

## Done when
The asset is earning referring domains and at least one internal link carries that authority toward
a commercial page.
