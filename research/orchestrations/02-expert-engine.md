## Goal
Earn editorial links by putting a credible person (founder / subject-matter expert) in front of
audiences that link back — roundups, press, and especially podcasts.

## When to run it
There's a willing expert with genuine authority who can show up publicly. Strongest technique per
Gotch, but bottlenecked by that person's availability.

## The sequence
1. **Pick the expert + topics** → [[strategies/founder-expert-led-link-building]] — _identify the figurehead and the subjects they can credibly speak on; this is the umbrella over guesting, hosting, roundups, and press._
2. **Guest on others' shows** → [[strategies/get-interviewed]] — _find podcasts/shows that link to guests (6 discovery methods), vet they actually link out, pitch with the cold/warm templates._
3. **Host your own show** → [[strategies/oprah-technique-podcast]] — _start a niche podcast and interview experts; the show + episodes earn links, and guests often link to their own episode (start with micro-influencers, scale up)._
4. **Get into existing expert roundups** → [[strategies/reverse-expert-roundups]] — _find already-ranking roundup posts and pitch to be added as a contributor, offering value (promotion/exposure) in return._
5. **Respond to journalist requests** → [[strategies/haro-journalist-requests]] — _answer Featured/HARO-style source requests daily; a numbers game (~25% pickup) that lands editorial/press links._
6. **Pitch on exposure** → [[strategies/outreach-value-proposition]] — _lead with the "exposure" value prop across all four channels — offer to promote their show/work to your audience._ Operate it with [[strategies/outreach-execution]] — _find/verify contacts, send, track, follow up._
7. **Recycle into assets** → [[strategies/linkable-asset-creation]] — _turn the expert's contributions (interview transcripts, roundup answers, quotes) into owned linkable assets, feeding the Asset Engine ([[orchestrations/01-asset-engine]])._

## Where the links point
Interview/show-note/press links usually point at your homepage or a bio/asset page. Where you have a
choice, point them at an asset and let the reverse-silo carry value onward (see [[strategies/reverse-silo]]).

## Done when
The expert is landing recurring placements that produce followed, editorial links on the hosts'
own websites (not just aggregator embeds).
