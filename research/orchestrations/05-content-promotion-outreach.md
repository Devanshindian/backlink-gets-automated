## Goal
Take a strong asset you already have and systematically place it on the pages that exist *to* link
out — roundups, "best of" lists, and resource pages — plus earn in-body links by contributing guest
content. This is the outreach arm the [[orchestrations/01-asset-engine]] hands off to once an asset
is live.

## When to run it
You have a genuinely link-worthy asset or blog and want to convert it into links through repeatable
outreach (not waiting for passive discovery). **Rule #1: only promote content that deserves it** —
promoting weak content burns the relationships you'll reuse.

## The sequence
1. **Build + rank the target list** → [[strategies/link-prospecting-prioritization]] — _gather candidate pages per channel (search strings + competitor reverse-engineering in Ahrefs/Semrush) and rank by relevance/authority._
2. **Pitch the recurring curators** → [[strategies/link-roundups]] — _get featured in weekly/periodic roundups (publishers already in a "link-out" mindset)._
3. **Get added to "best of" lists** → [[strategies/list-post-link-building]] — _ask to be added to top/best-X list posts; use Check My Links to find broken links on the list as leverage._
4. **Get listed on resource pages** → [[strategies/resource-page-link-building]] — _the "are you accepting submissions?" ask for curated resource pages (the get-listed angle; the broken-link/404 angle lives in [[orchestrations/03-replacement-outreach]])._
5. **Earn in-body links via guest content** → [[strategies/guest-posting]] — _pitch unique ideas, get approval first, then write and inject a natural in-body link to your asset._
6. **Pitch + operate** → [[strategies/outreach-value-proposition]] (the win-win framing) run through [[strategies/outreach-execution]] — _find/verify contacts, warm the inbox, send, track, follow up._ Keep anchors natural with [[strategies/anchor-text-optimization]].

## Where the links point
Most links point at the promoted **asset/blog post**. Let that asset pass value internally to money
pages via the reverse-silo (see [[strategies/reverse-silo]]). Keep guest-post body links pointed at
relevant assets, not commercial pages.

## Done when
The asset is steadily gaining referring domains from roundups, lists, resource pages, and guest-post
bodies — and the relationships are warm enough to reuse for the next asset.
