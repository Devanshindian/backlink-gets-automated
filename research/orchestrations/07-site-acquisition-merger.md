## Goal
Acquire a real, relevant website or tool that already has good backlinks and **301-redirect** it into
your site to inherit that link equity at once — instead of earning those links one at a time.

## When to run it
You have real budget and patience, and earning links the slow way isn't moving fast enough. This is
the highest-effort, highest-cost campaign here, with genuine downside risk if you pick the wrong
domain — treat it like acquiring a business, not buying a "link."

> **Warning (carried from the strategy):** do **not** use expired domains for this anymore — too risky.
> Only merge live, real assets you genuinely acquire.

## The sequence
1. **Find + vet targets** → [[strategies/merger-technique]] — _build a list of ≥100 acquirable tools/inactive blogs; vet each for a relevant, editorial link profile, under-optimized anchors, clean history (Archive.org), and index status._
2. **Sanity-check the link profile** → [[strategies/toxic-backlink-audit]] — _apply the toxic-link lens to the acquisition's backlinks; a profile full of spam/directory links is a reason to walk away, not buy._
3. **Negotiate + acquire** → [[strategies/merger-technique]] (Phases 1–2) operated via [[strategies/outreach-execution]] — _"interested in selling?" outreach, then value each link (DR 40–69 ≈ $200, DR 70 ≈ $300) and negotiate well below estimate._
4. **Build receiving pages if needed** → [[strategies/linkable-asset-creation]] — _where no relevant destination exists for an inherited link, create a strong page to receive it (Phase 3 fallback if the deal falls through: build a superior asset and chase the same linkers)._
5. **Merge + structure** → [[strategies/merger-technique]] (Phase 4) + [[strategies/reverse-silo]] — _map old→new URLs and 301 page-by-page to relevant destinations; route the inherited authority toward money pages._ Keep any new anchors natural with [[strategies/anchor-text-optimization]].

## Where the links point
Each 301 points an inherited link at the **most relevant destination page** on your site (never a
blanket redirect to the homepage — that looks unnatural). From there the reverse-silo carries value to
commercial pages.

## Done when
The acquired domain's referring domains now resolve (via 301) to relevant pages on your site, and
those pages show ranking lift (allow 4–8 weeks).
