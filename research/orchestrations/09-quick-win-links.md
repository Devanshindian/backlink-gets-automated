## Goal
Rack up legitimate backlinks with minimal effort and cost — register on high-authority platforms for
near-instant links, and seed original images that earn (and reclaim) credit links on autopilot. These
compound quietly in the background while engines 01–08 do the heavy lifting.

## When to run it
Anytime, as a low-effort background layer — especially valuable for a **new or thin backlink profile**
that needs early, diverse links fast. Low skill, low cost, low risk. Just don't mistake it for a
foundation: it supplements real asset/outreach work, it doesn't replace it.

## The sequence
1. **Register for fast platform links** → [[strategies/profile-link-building]] — _create profiles/entries on high-DA platforms: Favikon (do-follow, ~$29/mo), Grokipedia (do-follow wiki refs — get the entry indexed), and a Featured profile (no-follow, but shapes topical authority)._
2. **Seed images that earn links passively** → [[strategies/image-link-building]] — _make original photos/graphics, upload to DR-90+ free stock sites with your link in the attribution field, then reverse-image-search to reclaim uncredited uses (compounds; largely automatable)._
3. **Keep anchors natural** → [[strategies/anchor-text-optimization]] — _as these accumulate, keep anchor text branded/varied, not exact-match (guardrail)._

## Where the links point
Point profile and image-credit links at your homepage/brand and relevant money pages, with natural,
mostly branded anchors. The payoff is link volume + diversity; internally link any new authority into
the rest of the site (see [[strategies/reverse-silo]]).

## Done when
Profiles are created (and the Grokipedia entry is indexed), images are seeded and the first
reverse-image-search reclamation cycle is done — with new credit links continuing to appear on their own.

- [[strategies/anchor-text-optimization]] — keep anchors natural/branded as links accumulate (guardrail).
