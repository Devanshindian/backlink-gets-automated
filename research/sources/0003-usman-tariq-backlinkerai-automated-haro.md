## Summary
Hindi/Urdu YouTube walkthrough of **BacklinkerAI** (backlinker.ai), a tool that automates off-page
SEO by running the journalist-request ("HARO-style") play on autopilot. You create a one-time
**persona** (name, LinkedIn, company, job title, website, short bio); the tool then uses **Featured.com**
in the background to auto-pitch journalists/editors who are looking for expert sources, writing and
sending the pitch as your persona. Accepted pitches become published articles with an editorial
backlink to your site. The presenter shows ~2 months of his own results on gameofbranding.com.
**This is the existing [[strategies/haro-journalist-requests]] strategy, fully automated by a tool** —
not a new link-building method. The video is a promo (includes an affiliate discount code), so treat
the metrics/claims as vendor-supplied.

## Strategies extracted

## Tools mentioned
- **BacklinkerAI (backlinker.ai)** — the headline tool. "Get 65% more SEO traffic with outreach
  backlinks on autopilot." Guarantees ~3 backlinks; runs on a persona you set up once.
- **Featured (featured.com)** — what BacklinkerAI uses under the hood (a costly subscription the tool
  bundles); the journalist/source-request marketplace where pitches actually land.
- **Ahrefs** — used in the video to verify the Domain Rating (DR) of earned backlinks (Notion DR 92,
  RankTracker DR 77, etc.) and to show his own domain's DR rising 0 → 6.
- **ChatGPT** — suggested for writing the persona's professional bio.

- Earned links sat on genuinely high-authority domains: **DR 92, 77, 75, 70+** — placements that would
  cost "thousands of dollars" each if bought directly.
- Economics claimed: ~$300/mo (prorated $254), **50% off with code `letsuncover`** → ~$150/mo; at 17
  links ≈ **~$8 per backlink** vs. spammy Fiverr links.
- Framed as **"100% legit / white-hat"**: a real journalist finds a real subject-matter expert; the
  expert's free opinion increases the article's worth, and the link is the thank-you.

## Raw notes
- **What it replaces:** off-page SEO / manual backlink buying. On-page SEO is "already replaced" by AI
  writers; this targets off-page next.
- **Setup (one-time persona):** Configuration → Set up persona → Solo or Agency → first/last name,
  LinkedIn, company name, job title (e.g. Founder), company website, short professional bio (use
  ChatGPT). Presenter used "Game of Branding" (gameofbranding.com), persona "Sobhan/Subhan Tariq".
- **How it works:** journalists/editors/publishers on Featured post questions seeking expert input
  (e.g. "advertising trends in 2026"). BacklinkerAI auto-writes a short expert opinion as your persona,
  attaches your link/name/image, and submits. If the publisher accepts, they add it to their article
  with a backlink.
- **Dashboard sections:** Configuration (persona), Analytics (total-pitches trend, published count),
  Submitted/Links (per-month pitches), pitch states (approved / in review / selected / published).
- **Results shown:** total 17 backlinks across Dec+Jan; verified DRs in Ahrefs — Notion (redirects to
  notion.com) DR 92 / 24M backlinks / 130k linking sites; RankTracker DR 77; others 70–77. His own
  gameofbranding.com had no on-page SEO/articles yet, DR still moved 0 → 6 from these links alone.
- **Pricing:** ~$300 full month; prorated $254 mid-cycle (billing resets ~Feb 27 in his account);
  promo `letsuncover` = 50% off → ~$150 → ~$8/link.
- **Caveat (mine, not the video's):** vendor walkthrough with an affiliate coupon and a guaranteed-links
  promise. Metrics are self-reported. Treat "3 guaranteed / 65% more traffic / fully hands-off" as
  marketing; the underlying tactic is legitimate, but autopilot persona-pitching at volume carries the
  usual quality/relevance and authenticity risks — vet placements before relying on it for clients.
