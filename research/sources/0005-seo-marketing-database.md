## Summary
A reusable working database (6 tabs) of ready-to-apply link-building assets. Rather than feeding one
strategy, it's a **cross-cutting resource**: most strategy files now point to the relevant tab. Raw file
lives at `raw/Database - SEO & Marketing (1).xlsx`.

## The 6 tabs (what's in each + which strategies use it)
1. **Templates - Link Bait** — ~75 proven link-bait examples (Animagraffs animations, calculators,
   statistics roundups, facts listicles, generators, rankings, glossaries, maps, quizzes, visual/data
   pieces) each with a URL, **type tags**, and a fill-in **headline template** (e.g. "How [TOPIC]
   Works", "# [TOPIC] Statistics and Trends [Updated YEAR]", "We Analyzed [#] [TOPIC]. Here's What We
   Learned…"). → feeds [[strategies/link-bait-asset-types]], [[strategies/link-bait-generation-methods]],
   [[strategies/linkable-asset-creation]].
2. **Journalists & Copywriters** — ~110 contacts with email, website, LinkedIn/social, **topics**
   (heavily health, personal finance, SEO, tech, lifestyle), and **known publications** (Forbes,
   Healthline, Verywell, Investopedia, Bankrate, Search Engine Land, etc.). → feeds
   [[strategies/haro-journalist-requests]], [[strategies/get-interviewed]], [[strategies/guest-posting]],
   [[strategies/reverse-expert-roundups]], [[strategies/founder-expert-led-link-building]],
   [[strategies/outreach-execution]].
3. **Techniques - Link Building** — ~95 techniques scored on **Impact / Confidence / Ease → Total**
   (an ICE-style prioritization). Highest totals: Link Reclamation (brand mentions) 24; Lost-link
   reclamation 22; "Ask people you know" 22; Use social media 22; Create a blog 22. → feeds
   [[strategies/link-prospecting-prioritization]].
4. **Templates - Link Outreach** — 33 numbered email templates by link type. Key mapping:
   - **#1, #12, #28** Link Roundups → [[strategies/link-roundups]]
   - **#2, #29, #30** Resource Pages (incl. broken-link variant) → [[strategies/resource-page-link-building]]
   - **#3, #8, #9** Guest Post → [[strategies/guest-posting]]
   - **#4, #5, #6** Unlinked/мisspelled brand-mention reclamation (general outreach)
   - **#7** Image Reclamation → [[strategies/image-link-building]]
   - **#10, #11, #33** Shared-similar-content / Skyscraper, **#13** Outdated content → [[strategies/content-replacement]]
   - **#16–#21** Broken links → [[strategies/resource-page-link-building]]
   - **#22** Sponsored/advertising → [[strategies/buy-backlinks]]
   - **#24, #26** Local-blogger / competitor-link inserts → [[strategies/niche-edits]]
   - **#25, #32** Expert Roundups → [[strategies/reverse-expert-roundups]]
   - **#31** "Top blogs" list inclusion → [[strategies/list-post-link-building]]
   → master library for [[strategies/outreach-execution]] and [[strategies/outreach-value-proposition]].
5. **Resources - Data** — ~100 vetted **data-source URLs** grouped by category (general, employment,
   healthcare, economic/financial, crime, education, climate, marketing, etc. — e.g. data.gov,
   Pew Research, FRED, World Bank, CDC, Statista, BuzzSumo). → feeds
   [[strategies/link-bait-generation-methods]], [[strategies/linkable-asset-creation]].
6. **Templates - Search Strings** — hundreds of Google **search operators** grouped by link type:
   Donation, .edu/.gov, Become a Contributor, Guest Posts, Link Roundups, Lists, Niche Directories,
   Product Reviews, Resource Pages, Sponsored Posts, Sponsorships, Outdated Content, Forums, Blog
   Comments, Events. → feeds [[strategies/link-prospecting-prioritization]] and the "find targets" step of
   [[strategies/resource-page-link-building]], [[strategies/guest-posting]], [[strategies/link-roundups]],
   [[strategies/donation-link-building]], [[strategies/content-replacement]], [[strategies/content-resurrection]],
   [[strategies/list-post-link-building]], [[strategies/niche-business-associations]], [[strategies/buy-backlinks]].

## Notable / unique insights
- The link-bait tab encodes **headline templates**, not just examples — directly reusable for ideation.
- The techniques tab is effectively a **prebuilt ICE scoreboard** for choosing what to work on first.
- The search-strings tab covers nearly every prospecting motion in the KB in one place.

## Raw notes
- Contact DB skews **health** and **personal-finance** niches (and some SEO/tech) — most useful for
  sites in those verticals; treat names/emails as time-sensitive (verify before sending).
- No pricing/tooling content — this is a working-asset database, not a course.
- Full data preserved in the raw `.xlsx`; this entry is the index + cross-links.
