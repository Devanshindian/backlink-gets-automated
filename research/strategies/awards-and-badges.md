## 1. What it is
Create an awards or "best of" program and give winners a **badge** (an image, e.g. "Top 10 Web
Developers 2023") they can embed on their own site — and that badge links back to you.

In plain terms: you become the authority that *hands out* recognition. Recipients proudly display the
badge (often on their homepage), and each embed is a relevant backlink to you. Clutch.co does this at
scale, earning links on thousands of highly-relevant agency sites.

## 2. Why it works
- Recipients *want* to display the award (social proof), so they link to you voluntarily — at scale.
- Badges land on highly relevant, often homepage, placements (a web-dev award on web-dev agency sites).
- It positions your brand as an industry authority, compounding trust and future links.

## 3. When to use / prerequisites
- You have (or can build) enough brand credibility for an award to feel meaningful.
- You can design badges and a page explaining the methodology/winners.
- A directory/ratings angle helps (the Clutch / Expertise / Design Rush model).

## 4. End-to-end process
1. **Decide the award category and methodology** — e.g. "Top 3 Website Design Companies in [City]",
   "50 Best [NICHE] Companies". Use [[tools/tools#chatgpt]] to brainstorm award concepts (the SOP's
   example prompt: "I want to create a link bait campaign like clutch.co… Give me 10 ideas for a plumber
   in St Louis" → e.g. "St. Louis Plumbing Innovation Awards"). _tool(s): chatgpt_
2. **Select winners** and create a results/methodology page on your site.
3. **Design embeddable badges** with the embed code pointing back to your winners page (branded anchor).
4. **Notify winners** and make it easy to embed the badge (provide the snippet).
5. Track embeds and refresh annually (badges are often year-stamped, e.g. "2023", which prompts renewals).

## 5. Tools
- [[tools/tools#chatgpt]] — brainstorm award categories and angles per niche/city.

## 6. Success metrics
- Number of badge embeds live (= referring domains), and how many are homepage placements.
- Relevance/authority of recipient sites; year-over-year renewal embeds.

## 7. Risks & pitfalls
- **Meaningless awards** — if the award has no credibility, few will embed it; invest in real methodology.
- **Exact-match anchors in the badge** — keep the badge link branded/generic
  ([[strategies/anchor-text-optimization]]).
- **High upfront effort** — this is an advanced, brand-led play, not a quick win.
